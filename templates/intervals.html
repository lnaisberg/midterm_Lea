Intervals

An interval is the distance between two notes.
It possesses a quality and a number.
To determine the number just count the letters between the notes, including the starting and ending points.
The quality depends on the number. There are two groups of qualities:
  • Perfect Intervals: Unisons, 4ths, and 5ths
  • Imperfect Intervals: 2nds, 3rds, 6ths, and 7ths
